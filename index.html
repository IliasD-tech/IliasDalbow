About

I'm a Marketing student at the University of Delaware who got into social media by accident and never looked back. I started managing content for The Picklr Newark and it just clicked. Now I run social media for two clients across Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube, generating 300K+ views a month.

01 — Listen first
What are you trying to say
I start by asking questions. What do you want this content to do, what are you trying to say, and how do you want people to feel when they see it.
02 — Define the goal
Revenue, reach, or awareness
Content without a clear goal is just noise. I want to know what success looks like before anything gets made — whether that's revenue, reach, impressions, or something else.
03 — Build together
Communicating throughout
I bring options to the table and talk through them. I stay in communication at every step so nothing comes back wrong and the final product actually reflects what they were going for.
